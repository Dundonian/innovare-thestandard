Looking for a Lower Queen Anne apartment for rent - unfurnished or furnished? The Standard offers the location, charm, functionality, and boutique atmosphere you desire.

When you want to get away from the noise of the city, but still want to be just a FEW blocks away from cafés, shops, groceries, transportation, entertainment, scenic walks, employment and …come home to The Standard.

Located on a quite street, surrounded by exceptional views of the Puget Sound and the Space Needle, The Standard is a residential solace within the city. A place to enjoy charming and spacious homes, a place you can walk everywhere, a place your pet will love, a place to get to know your neighbors relaxing in the open-air courtyard or over a friendly game of outdoor ping pong. The Standard is a place you will want to call HOME.

  • Amazing views of Elliot Bay and the Space Needle
  • Quartz counter-tops with tile back-splash
  • Hardwood flooring
  • Plenty of storage and closet space
  • On-site limited reserved parking
  • Laundry center
  • Community garden
  • Outdoor Ping Pong Table
  • Pet friendly
  • Flexible Lease Terms
