Neighborhood

The Standard enjoys a spectacular location in Lower Queen Anne on 5th Ave West, with nearby access to:

  • Restaurants: Toulouse Petit, Taylor Shellfish Oyster Bar, Peso’s
  • Coffee Shops: Uptown Espresso, Café Ladro, Caffee Zingaro, KEXP 90.3 FM
  • Groceries: The Metropolitan Market, Safeway, Bartell Drug or CVS
  • Entertainment & Recreation: SIFF Cinema Uptown, Seattle Science Center, Key Arena, Kerry Park, Myrtle Edwards Park
  • Employment Centers: ~ 1 mile from South Lake Union, ~ 2 miles from Seattle CBD
  • Transportation: 4 min walk to high frequency bus stop (Rapid Ride)
